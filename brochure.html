About 02

What is
Stratum?

Stratum is an evidence and intelligence advisory built for life sciences organizations operating in complex, high-stakes environments. We work at the intersection of science, data, and strategy — helping biotech, precision medicine, and healthcare companies transform fragmented information into clear, decision-ready intelligence.

In today's landscape, the challenge is not lack of data — it is the absence of clarity. Organizations are inundated with publications, competitor filings, pipeline trackers, and market reports. Yet the path forward often remains opaque.

Stratum exists to solve that. We synthesize the right signals, frame them in strategic context, and deliver intelligence that enables confident action — not further deliberation.

"The challenge is not lack of data — it is the absence of clarity."

Focused Intelligence Briefs
